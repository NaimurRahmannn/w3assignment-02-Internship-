About

Rediscover Sanctuary Cap Cana, the Dominican Republic’s most exclusive adults-only all-inclusive resort. You’ll be delighted by the resort’s $45 million modernization as you explore spectacular suites, artfully crafted lobbies, updated restaurants and bars, enhanced world-class spa, and state-of-the-art fitness center.

Sanctuary Cap Cana, A Luxury Collection Adult All-Inclusive Resort is located in Cap Cana, a private gated community retreat inside Punta Cana. Cap Cana is made up of 30,000 acres of flawless beaches...

Enjoy sweeping ocean vistas, intimate beach lounges, and a marina district filled with boutiques and dining. The resort blends timeless Caribbean architecture with modern comforts, delivering serene suites, private pools, and sunset terraces designed for quiet escape. Experience curated culinary journeys, attentive butler service, and wellness rituals that turn every moment into a restorative retreat.
